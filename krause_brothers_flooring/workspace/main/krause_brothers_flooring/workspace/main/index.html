  • Installation

    Do you need a new floor installed? At Krause Brothers Flooring, we excel at installing real hardwood floors. You will be pleased with our quick service coupled with quality work. If you desire other "wood" options such as engineered or laminate we can do that too. Let Krause Brothers Flooring help you make the best decision that suits your home or business.

  • Refinishing

    Do your hardwood floors need a makeover? At Krause Brothers Flooring, we pride ourselves on offering the best sanding and refinishing process in Central Ohio. Our process is virtually dustless thanks to our high-quality, built-in vacuum system. We, then, only use the best products to finish your floors. The premium waterbourne finishes have minimal odor while providing a durable hardwood finish. Krause Brothers Flooring will restore your hardwood floors to their original luster.

  • Clean

    Are your floors dirty and just need a good deep clean? You may just need a professional clean and recoat by Krause Brothers Flooring. This process is quick and easy and will also even eliminate minor scuffs in your floors. During this process we go over it twice to make sure it is clean. Then, we put a commercial grade polyurethane down.
